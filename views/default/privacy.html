0. Introduction

In brief: 2 K SERVICE protects your data. This policy explains how, for both the website and the SaaS app.

The company 2 K SERVICE (sole proprietor / micro-business), operating the YourFirstShip platform (“we”, “us”, “our”), is committed to protecting your personal data.

This Privacy & Cookie Policy (“Policy”) describes our practices regarding the collection, use, sharing, and protection of information when you use:

  • the marketing website yourfirstship.com; and
  • the YourFirstShip SaaS application (dashboard, editor, hosting, runtime, support) (together, the “Service”).

By using the Service, you agree to this Policy and our Terms of Service.

1. Definitions

  • Customer / Direct User: a legal entity or professional (B2B) who creates a YourFirstShip account and uses the editor to design / generate / host applications.
  • End User: any person who uses an application created by a Customer (e.g., the Customer’s clients, students, patients, prospects).
  • Account Data: data related to the Customer’s account (identity, access, billing, logs, support).
  • Project Data: prompts (inputs), generated code (outputs), database schemas, files, “Time Machine” versions, configurations, and technical logs related to the project.
  • Subprocessor (GDPR meaning): a provider processing data on our behalf (hosting, payments, AI, etc.).

3. GDPR roles: Controller vs Processor

3.1 Customer data (B2B)

For Account Data (account creation, authentication, billing, support, security), 2 K SERVICE acts as the Data Controller.

3.2 End Users’ data in generated apps

For data processed within applications created by our Customers (End Users’ data), the Customer generally acts as the Data Controller and YourFirstShip acts as a Data Processor under the GDPR.

The Customer is responsible for:

  • providing required notices to their End Users;
  • their own privacy policy;
  • the legal basis and purposes of processing within their application; and
  • overall compliance of their application and its content.

YourFirstShip access to such data:

  • by default, access is limited to technical logs required for hosting and security; and
  • access to End User content occurs only upon the Customer’s explicit request (support) or in case of critical technical intervention (maintenance, security, incident).

End User export / deletion: dashboard features enable the Customer to export/delete their End Users’ data (depending on the app configuration).

A) Account data (B2B)

5. Purposes and legal bases

We process your data only when necessary, including to:

  • Perform the contract (Terms / subscription): create and secure your account, provide the editor, AI generation, hosting, maintenance, and support.
  • Comply with legal obligations: invoicing, accounting, retention of certain logs.
  • Legitimate interests: security, fraud prevention, product improvement (internal analytics), B2B service communications.
  • Consent (where required): non-essential cookies/trackers, marketing newsletter subscription.
