Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know to start with peace of mind.

Concept & Ownership

Do I need to know how to code?
Absolutely not. YourFirstShip is designed for business experts, not engineers. If you can explain your business to a colleague, you can build your app. Our AI handles the technical translation (Database, API, Security).
Do I really own the code? (No Lock-in?)

Yes, that's our major difference. Unlike other 'black box' platforms, you are not a tenant of your technology.

You can click "Eject" at any time to download a ZIP containing:

  • Clean source code (Python/Brython).
  • Your database structure.
  • A Docker container ready to be hosted elsewhere.

Web & Mobile

What is a "PWA" vs a "Native App"?

We use PWA (Progressive Web App) technology, the modern standard used by Twitter, Uber, or Starbucks.

Advantages:

  • No need to pay the 30% commission to Apple/Google.
  • No waiting for Store validation.
  • One-click installation via link (QR Code or URL).
  • Works on iPhone, Android, PC, and Mac with a single version.
Do I have to pay twice for Web and Mobile?
No! That's the power of our "Holodeck" architecture. You build a single project, and it adapts automatically. Your data, users, and logic are shared between the desktop version (Admin) and mobile version (Client). Everything is included in the single plan.

Pricing & Credits

What is an "AI Action"?

An "Action" is when you ask the AI to work for you. For example:

  • "Create a login page" = 1 Action.
  • "Change button color to blue" = 1 Action.
  • "Generate database schema" = 1 Action.

Note that usage of your application by your clients (visits, clicks) does not consume your AI credits.

What happens if I exceed my quota?

Unlike other tools, your application does not stop. It remains online, and your clients can use it.

It is your ability to modify the app with AI that is paused until the next month. If you are in a rush, you can buy a Top-up (Booster Pack) of 250 actions for â‚¬10, valid immediately.
